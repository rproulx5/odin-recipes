Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipehttps://joyfoodsunshine.com/the-most-amazing-chocolate-chip-cookies/

This is a recipe to make the most delicious chocolate chip cookies known to humans. We hope you enjoy this recipe.

A simple recipe handed down from generations, it only requires basic ingredients, please see below.

Ingredients

    1 cup of salted butter
    1 cup of white granulated sugar
    1 cup of light brown sugar
    2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
    2 large eggs
    3 cups of all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon of baking soda
    1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
    1 teaspoon of sea salt
    2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

<0l>Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
    In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. Set aside.
    Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
    Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
    Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
    Add 12 oz package of chocolate chips and mix well.
    Roll 2-3 TBS (depending on how large you like your cookies) of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets. (alternately, use a small cookie scoop to make your cookies).
    Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just BARELY starting to turn brown.
    Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
